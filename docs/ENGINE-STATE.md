### Swim clean per-length / per-segment pace is UNRECOVERABLE from Strava + the Garmin webhook — PROVEN 2026-06-18, DO NOT RE-CHASE (D-200)

(`_garmin-native-verify`, `_garmin-samples-check`, `_garmin-active-time`)
